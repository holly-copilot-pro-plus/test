## About Elephants

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth! They are known for:

- Their long trunks (which are actually elongated noses)
- Large ears that help regulate body temperature
- Impressive memory and intelligence
- Strong family bonds and social structures
- Tusks made of ivory (modified incisor teeth)
